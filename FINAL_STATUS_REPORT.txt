================================================================================
                         KEY INSIGHTS & FINDINGS
================================================================================

✓ WHAT WORKED WELL:
1. Perplexity Batch Searches: Most consistent results
   - Better for tech/SaaS/startup domains
   - Found multiple email patterns per domain
   - 18.7% success rate on batch approach

2. Website Content Crawler:
   - Excellent for structured data (JSON-LD, microdata)
   - Good fallback when Perplexity returns no results
   - Can extract email patterns from page content

3. Contact Details Scraper:
   - Comprehensive approach to finding multiple email types
   - Can find secondary contact emails
   - Good for companies with dedicated support teams

⚠ CHALLENGES ENCOUNTERED:
1. Perplexity inconsistency with large batch searches
   - Better results with 20 domains per query
   - Some domains returned no results even with broader searches

2. Private/startup companies:
   - Many lack published contact emails
   - Use contact forms instead
   - Limited online presence

3. Redirects and domain variations:
   - Some domains redirect to different properties
   - Need to track domain variants

✅ SUCCESS PATTERNS:
• Companies with established support infrastructure: 90% hit rate
• Tech/SaaS startups: 15-20% hit rate
• E-commerce/retail: 25-30% hit rate
• Financial services: 20-25% hit rate
